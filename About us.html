Modesire's Day Nursery is a purpose built nursery in Clayton area, built with care and development of children as highest priority. Built into a modern bright and inspiring day nursery with a lot of parking, our nursery is sure to be the right choice for you and your child. We are committed to providing and giving the best quality of care, led by a strong-willed dedicated team, who are challenged by each other to think, set goals and develop in continuing to better ourselves. Our committment gave us the driving force behind everything we do, with our achievement through team works and pride in individual acomplishment which contribute to our overall success.

Our aim is to provide a high quality childcare services making sure that your child will be in a warm, professional,secure, loving and caring environment.


Inspiration
